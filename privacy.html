Effective Date: 1 March 2026   |   Compliant with IT Act 2000 & DPDP Act 2023

At SRT Digital Solutions, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

SRT Digital Solutions is a digital marketing agency based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. We provide social media marketing, local SEO, Google Business Profile management, and digital growth services for doctors and healthcare practices.

Data Controller: SRT Digital Solutions
Email: growwithus@srtdigitalsolutions.com
Location: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out our contact form, book a consultation, or engage our services, we may collect:

  • Full name and professional designation (e.g., Dr.)
  • Clinic name and address
  • Email address and phone number
  • Medical specialty and practice details
  • Business challenges and goals shared during consultations

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect limited technical data:

  • IP address (anonymized for analytics)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (city level)

2.3 Information from Social Media Platforms

If you grant us access to manage your social media accounts, we may access:

  • Account analytics and performance metrics
  • Follower demographics and engagement data
  • Ad account performance data

We access only what is necessary to deliver the agreed services and do not store this data beyond the active engagement period.

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Legal Basis Data Used
Respond to consultation requests Consent / Contract Name, email, phone, specialty
Deliver contracted marketing services Contract All client business data
Send service updates and reports Contract Email, phone
Send marketing communications (with opt-in) Consent Email, phone
Website analytics and improvement Legitimate interest Anonymized usage data
Legal compliance and record keeping Legal obligation Business and financial data

We will never use your personal data for purposes other than those listed above without obtaining your explicit consent first.

4. Patient Data

We do not collect, store, or process any patient health information (PHI) or patient personal data. We manage your clinic's digital presence — not your patient records. Any patient-facing content we create is reviewed and approved by you before publication.

Clients are reminded that sharing identifiable patient data (photos, testimonials, case details) with us for marketing requires the patient's informed written consent under applicable medical ethics guidelines and the DPDP Act 2023.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

5.1 What We Use

Our website uses minimal cookies to function and improve your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (session management, form security)
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics to understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference cookies: Remember settings such as language or form pre-fill

5.2 Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include integrations from third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager — anonymized traffic analysis
  • Meta Pixel — if used for retargeting campaigns (only with your consent)

5.3 Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service delivery: With trusted tools and platforms (Google Workspace, project management tools) used to deliver your services, under confidentiality agreements
  • Platform access: Credentials shared with our team members who work on your accounts are protected and access-controlled
  • Legal requirement: When required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfer: If SRT Digital Solutions undergoes a merger or acquisition, you will be notified and your data will transfer under the same privacy protections

7. Data Storage and Security

7.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is primarily stored on servers located in India or countries that provide an equivalent level of data protection. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) for form data storage, which complies with industry-standard security practices.

7.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized team members only
  • Regular security reviews of our tools and systems
  • Secure password management and two-factor authentication on all platforms

7.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Enquiry data (non-clients): 12 months from date of enquiry
  • Client data: Duration of contract plus 3 years for legal/financial record-keeping
  • Analytics data: 26 months (standard analytics retention)

After these periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights Under Indian Law

Under the Information Technology Act 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate a person to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity (under DPDP Act)
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with us or the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at growwithus@srtdigitalsolutions.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Marketing Communications

If you have provided your email or phone number, we may send you relevant updates about our services, healthcare marketing tips, and industry insights — but only with your consent.

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email we send
  • Replying "STOP" to any WhatsApp or SMS message
  • Emailing us at growwithus@srtdigitalsolutions.com

Opting out of marketing does not affect service-related communications (invoices, reports, important account notifications).

10. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites including social media platforms, Google, and other resources. Once you leave our website, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adult healthcare professionals only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify active clients via email
  • Display a notice on our website where appropriate

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Contact Us & Grievance Officer

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Grievance Officer:

  • Grievance Officer: SRT Digital Solutions Management Team
  • Email: growwithus@srtdigitalsolutions.com
  • Address: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Response Time: Within 30 days of receiving your complaint

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may escalate your complaint to the Data Protection Board of India once operational under the DPDP Act 2023.
